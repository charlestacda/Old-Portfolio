About Me

Greetings! My name is Charles Joshua Tacda, a 19 years old College student from the Philippines. I’m a video editor, audio editor, website designer, and reviewer. I love watching movies, anime, and other content creators on YouTube and Twitch.


I have been editing videos for the past six years. I later expanded my skills in other things like editing audio and creating a website design.


I was born on November 25, 2001, and raised in General Trias, Cavite, Philippines. I am quite an introvert and like doing things alone. I like simple things when it comes to my clothes and food. I believe in the saying, "Time is the most precious thing in life".

My father and mother raised me with my two older brothers. Our father, Vincent Tacda, is a hard-working man who is trying his best to provide income to support us. Meanwhile, our mother, Nilda Tacda, is the one who takes care of us at home. The first child and eldest brother, Earl Stephen Tacda, is a smart person who likes to read manga and develop games. The second child John Michael Tacda, likes to draw and sing in the choir.

I studied in our subdivision, Parklane, when I was in elementary grade. I then went into three different schools when I was in high school. First was in Maranatha Christian Academy School in Imus when I was still in 7th grade. I then transfer to Dasmariñas Integrated High School Main, where I was in a Sports Academy section in my 8th and 9th grade. I then quit Sports and went into the academic in my 10th grade. Lastly, in my 11th and 12th grade, I enrolled in Lyceum of the Philippines Cavite. As of right now, I am currently a 2nd-year college student who is studying under a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology program.

My hobbies are watching anime, tv shows, movies, and youtube videos. I like to consume different kinds of stories through these media. I learned a lot of things just by watching something on the internet. I also took an interest in editing video and audio as I was inspired by the movies and shows that I have watched. In my elementary and high school days, I played sports like chess and lawn tennis. Although I already quitted on playing lawn tennis for the extra curriculum, I still have a passion for playing sports with my friends like badminton and table tennis.
